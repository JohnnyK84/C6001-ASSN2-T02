Help Information

  1. LOCATION SEARCH: To find the location of your choice, simply type the name of the city or town, or nearest urban centre, and press enter. You can also select from the list of completion options that appear as you type.

  2. WEATHER: The weather information updates to the current weather for each location selected. It includes the city name, the weather description, the temperature and the windspeed for that location.

  3. ZOOM FUNCTION: Use the plus [+] and minus [-] buttons on the right of each map to zoom in or to zoom out.

  4. STREET VIEW: To view the locality at street level place the mouse cursor - it turns into a small hand - on the yellow figure at the right of the map, then click and drag the figure to a location on the map.

  5. SATELLITE VIEW: To switch to Satellite View and see the geographical terrain of the locality, select the Satellite option near the top left hand corner of the map.

  6. FORECAST: To see a 5 day weather forecast for the same location, select the 5 Day Forecast button underneath the Weather Information.
